Polishee

I am the sole designer on a 5-person team. We cooperate with a hardware company to build an app for people to paint any images on their nails.

Role

User Research

Brand Design

Interface Design

App Development

Length

8 months

Year

2018-19

About This Product

This app allows users to choose images from our library or upload from album and preview on a mock hand to see the result of nail arts.

Key Features

1. Pick or upload images

Users can pick any images from the image library we provided or upload photos from their album.

2. Preview

Second, users can put images on an image of real hands to simulate the nail arts.

3. Link and Print

After previewing, users can link the app to our nail-printer via Wi-Fi and print images on nails in only a few seconds.

User and Audience

The target users are young girls interesting in nail arts and passionating about DIY at home. These users like innovation products and devices about fashion and beauty that could make them have some topics with their homie.

From 0 to 1

We delivered a questionnaire to know the market of this product field and made a persona to make sure that every member of our team had the same mental picture of our target audience.

Wireframes

Mockups

For smoothly experience, I designed a component called Palette to store and show images that user chose. They could quickly store images they liked and put them on the mock screen to simulate the nail arts on their fingers. Then send these images to the nail printer to have real custom-picture nail art.

Logo Design

I designed a logo font for Polishee - about creative, fashion, revolution and beauty. That was my first time to design about typograhpy and fonts.

Outcomes

The app was well received within our team and client. I'm also an engineer developing this project with React Native. While it has not yet been released, it has already worked with the test nail printer.
