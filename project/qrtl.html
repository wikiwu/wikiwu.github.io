Q-Rental

I was a product designer on a three-person mock startup team.

We created Q-Rental, a service for daily rental suite holder and passenger that made them have a better experience in the transaction process.

Role

Service Design

Contextual Inquiry

Length

3 months

Year

2015

About This Project

This product of service provides a method for landlords and tenants with an IoT smart lock.

For landlords, this system integrates daily rental suite transaction behavior processes. They can manage any rooms remotely by the app.

For tenants, they can visit the app and book the room they want in the app. After the order set up, they can visit to the room and unlock the smart lock by app.

User Scenarios

1.Search for room type

The operators put rented information on the platform to make it easier for visitors to search, browse and compare.

2. Third party payment

Consumers use the APP platform to connect with credit cards to complete a fast and secure payment process, as well as to secure transactions between the two parties.

3. Smart lock

After confirming the payment, the passenger can obtain the door lock management authority without waiting for the consumer, personally bringing the position and delivering the key.

4. Rating System

The tenant and the landlord share the accommodation and give an evaluation, and provide objective opinions of the follow-up landlord and the tenant.
